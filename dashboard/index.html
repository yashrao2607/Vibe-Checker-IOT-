Vibe-Checker: Crowd Mood Analysis Conclusion

93% Accuracy Real-time Audio IoT Edge Hub

Detailed Conclusion: The successful implementation of the Vibe-Checker: Digital Crowd Mood Estimator represents a significant advancement in crowd monitoring technology. This project effectively bridges the gap between raw acoustic hardware sensing and high-level behavioral analytics.


By leveraging an IoT Edge Microcontroller (ESP32/Arduino) together with sophisticated Random Forest Ensemble models, we have demonstrated that mass crowd behavior can be accurately predicted by analyzing noise variance and peak intensity. The system's ability to differentiate between focused silence, group enjoyment, and chaotic shouting proves its utility in smart infrastructure management and automated event safety.
